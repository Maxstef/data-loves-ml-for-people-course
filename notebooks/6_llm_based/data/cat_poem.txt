--- POEM ---

Title: Whiskers and Paws

In shadows cast by moonlit beams,
Cats dance in enigmatic dreams,
With softest fur and eyes that gleam,
They move with grace, a silent stream.

With lazy yawns in sunlight's glow,
They nap where gentle breezes blow,
And chase their tails or toys in glee,
Embarking on night hunts with glee.

A purr that soothes a troubled mind,
A feline friend, loyal and kind,
With quirky ways and playful flair,
They weave a bond beyond compare.

In ancient tales, a mystic guise,
They tread where ancient magic lies,
Free spirits with a noble air,
A whiskered presence everywhere.

Celebrate the cat's unique charm,
A bond that holds us safe and warm,
For in their eyes, we often see,
The wild, untamed, and truly free.
